## What is Buji?

Buji is a **Personal AI Tutor** built into stibe's online learning platform. Unlike generic AI chatbots, Buji **knows your child** — their strengths, weaknesses, exam scores, classroom behavior, and learning style. It's like having a brilliant, infinitely patient tutor available round the clock.

> *"An AI that never sleeps, never forgets, and gets smarter every day."*

---

## Key Features

### 🎓 Personal Academic Coach

- **Solves doubts instantly** — type, speak, or photograph any question from textbook or homework
- **Step-by-step explanations** — not just answers, but the *why* behind every solution
- **Learns from mistakes** — reviews wrong answers from exams and teaches the right approach
- **Curriculum-aligned** — CBSE, ICSE, ISC, and State Board syllabi built in
- **All subjects covered** — Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Hindi, Social Science

### 📊 Smart Performance Tracking

- **Knows every exam result** — question-level analysis of what went right and wrong
- **Tracks strengths & weaknesses** — "You're great at Trigonometry but need practice in Probability"
- **Detects trends** — "Your Chemistry scores improved 15% over the last 3 exams!"
- **Attention insights** — uses AI classroom monitoring to identify focus patterns

### 📅 Personalised Study Planner

- **Custom study schedules** — based on exam dates, weak subjects, and available hours
- **Revision reminders** — spaced repetition so your child never forgets what they've learned
- **Homework tracker** — "You have 2 pending assignments. Chemistry is due tomorrow."
- **Pre-class preparation** — quick refreshers before every live session

### 🎤 Talk, Type, or Snap — Buji Understands

- **Voice input** — speak your questions, hands-free
- **Voice responses** — Buji reads answers aloud for revision on the go
- **Photo questions** — snap a photo of any problem — handwritten, textbook, or whiteboard
- **PDF analysis** — upload study material and get instant summaries and practice questions

### 🔔 Proactive — Buji Reaches Out First

- **Class reminders** — "Your Chemistry class starts in 30 minutes!"
- **Low attendance alerts** — "You've missed 3 classes this month. Let's fix this!"
- **Post-exam follow-up** — "You scored 40% on Maths. Want me to go through your wrong answers?"
- **Motivational nudges** — "You improved 12% in Chemistry this month! Keep going!"
- **Fee & credit reminders** — so nothing falls through the cracks

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Built for Parents Too

- **Daily updates** — "How was my child's day at stibe?" — one message, full picture
- **Exam results** — instant notifications with detailed breakdowns
- **Attendance tracking** — know when your child joins, misses, or is late
- **Weekly reports** — auto-generated digest covering academics, behavior, and attendance
- **Fee management** — check pending fees and make payments directly
